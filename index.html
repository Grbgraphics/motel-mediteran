About Us

The closest place to stay near the San Diego Safari Park is the Motel Mediteran! Completely renovated with beautifully decorated rooms, a smoke free environments and great amenities. You'll rest soundly after a hard day of play, from golfing, shopping, epic outdoor activities which are all within minutes of our great location. The Motel Mediteran is a great place to stay!
